Duration: Sep 2023 - Dec 2024

In the GreenShield Plus capstone project, I played a pivotal role in enhancing the functionality of a healthcare application by introducing innovative features to improve user engagement and predictive health capabilities. The focus was on integrating advanced analytics and machine learning models to deliver personalized health insights and actionable recommendations.

Key contributions included designing and implementing the BreathWell feature for real-time pulmonary health monitoring. Leveraging audio-based data, such as breath and cough sounds, I utilized advanced signal processing techniques and machine learning algorithms, including Random Forest and Gradient Boosting, to assess respiratory health. This feature enabled users to monitor key metrics like breathing frequency, airflow volume, and cough intensity, categorized into health risk levels for actionable feedback.

My responsibilities encompassed:

Data Collection: Conducted extensive data collection through user input, simulations, and real-time audio recordings, ensuring diverse and high-quality datasets for model training.
Predictive Analytics: Developed models to analyze user data, employing feature engineering techniques such as spectral centroid analysis and Short-Time Fourier Transform (STFT).
User-Centric Dashboards: Designed interactive dashboards using Power BI to provide users with health insights and trends, tailored to demographic and lifestyle factors.
Collaboration: Worked closely with cross-functional teams to align technical solutions with user needs and business objectives.
